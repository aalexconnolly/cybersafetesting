TALKING TECH

If you are only going to take one thing away from this campaign, it should be this. It is so important to have open conversations
open conversations with your children about their online activity.

IT DOESN’T MATTER WHAT THE QUESTION IS, JUST START THE CONVERSATION.

START TALKING

Talking with your children about what they are doing online should become a part of everyday life. Asking them small questions like “What do you like doing most online?”, “What can go wrong?” and “What could you do?” can be great starting points. Just as in real life, talk to them about what is okay and not okay to do online.
