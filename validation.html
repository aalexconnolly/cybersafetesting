ONLINE VALIDATION

One of the bad things about the internet is social media . It’s not neccesarily all bad but
it is making children and even adults crave a sense of validation from the things they post. Which is not good!

LIKES, LIKES & MORE LIKES

Most social media platforms have some sort of ‘like’ system. This type of system affects children’s self esteem and leads them to crave a sense of validation from their peers and even strangers online. Some children can even begin to measure their self-worth from this.
