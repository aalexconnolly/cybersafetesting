BAFFLED? is a campaign by CyberSafeIreland to help parents un-baffle themselves when it comes to online safety for their children. This content, developed with the support of IADT Visual Communication students, contains some of the most important information that parents should focus on, but there is a lot more... Check out our website to become an expert on how to be Stronger, Smarter and Safer online.

CYBERSAFEIRELAND

CyberSafeIreland is an Irish-registered charity that aims to empower children and parents to navigate the online world in a safe and responsible manner. When it comes to technology, we want Irish children to be #StrongerSmarterSafer online.

WHAT WE DO

Education is key. We give talks to schools, children, parents and corporates. To date, we have spoken to thousands of children aged between 8 and 13, as well as many of their parents. We primarily visit primary schools, but also given talks or training sessions to corporates, sports clubs, children's charities and gardai. Find out more
